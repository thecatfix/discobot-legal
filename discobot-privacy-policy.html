DiscoBot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 12, 2025

Important: DiscoBot is a test/educational bot. This privacy policy describes our data practices for learning and development purposes.

1. Information We Collect

DiscoBot may collect the following types of information when you interact with it:

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Discord User ID: Your unique Discord identifier
  • Username and Display Name: Your Discord username and server nickname
  • Server Information: Guild ID, channel ID where commands are used
  • Message Content: Content of messages that directly mention or command the bot
  • Timestamps: When interactions occur

Command Data:

  • Commands sent to the bot
  • Command parameters and arguments
  • Response data and interaction results

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Bot Functionality: Processing commands and providing responses
  • Development and Testing: Improving bot features and debugging
  • Learning: Understanding Discord API workflows and bot development
  • Analytics: Basic usage statistics for development purposes

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Information may be shared only in these circumstances:

  • Technical Requirements: Data necessary for Discord API functionality
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Development Purposes: Anonymized data for learning and improvement

4. Data Retention

We retain information as follows:

  • Temporary Data: Command interactions may be logged temporarily for debugging
  • Development Data: Anonymized usage patterns may be retained for learning purposes
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not maintain permanent databases of user conversations

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect information, including:

  • Secure API token management using 1Password
  • Limited access to bot systems and data
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Compliance with Discord's security requirements

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have collected
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to technical limitations)
  • Opt-out: Stop using the bot or remove it from your server
  • Questions: Contact us about our data practices

7. Third-Party Services

DiscoBot operates through Discord's platform and is subject to:

  • Discord Privacy Policy: https://discord.com/privacy
  • Discord Terms of Service: https://discord.com/terms
  • Discord Developer Terms: Developer Terms

8. Children's Privacy

DiscoBot is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information.

9. International Users

DiscoBot may be used by users in different countries. By using the bot, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this privacy policy.
